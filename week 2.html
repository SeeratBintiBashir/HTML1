Amar Singh COLLEGE
Amar Singh College, Srinagar is a leading College of Srinagar.It has an enrollment of more than 5000 students. It offers four year undergraduate programmes in History, Computer Science, Zoology and many other subjects.
Sri Pratap COLLEGE
Sri Pratap College, Srinagar is a reputed College of Srinagar.It is a constituent College of Cluster University Srinagar. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programmes in Science only.
